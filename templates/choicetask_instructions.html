Choice Instructions

Two food items will be shown on the screen in each trial. One item will be on the left and the second item will be on the right. Please determine which food item you prefer more. If you prefer the food item on the left, press key "u". If you prefer the food item on the right, press key "i". If your response is recorded, the box at the center of the screen will momentarily turn green. If you respond too late or do not respond, the box will momentarily turn red before moving onto the next trial.
